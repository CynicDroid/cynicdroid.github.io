USE OF COOKIES

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, maintain your sessions, and improve website performance. They allow us to:

  • Keep you logged in and remember your preferences
  • Analyze traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content
  • Deliver relevant advertisements

TYPES OF COOKIES

  • Advertising Cookies: Display relevant ads and track ad performance without collecting personal info.
  • Analytics Cookies: Monitor site usage and improve features.
  • First-Party Cookies: Necessary for site functionality.
  • Personalization Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.
  • Security Cookies: Authenticate users and protect data.
  • Site Management Cookies: Maintain sessions and retain input data.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Cookies from external services used on our site.

CONTROL OF COOKIES

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionalities. Additionally, you can opt-out of some third-party cookies via the Network Advertising Initiative’s Opt-Out Tool.
